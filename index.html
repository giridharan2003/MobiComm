Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Mobi Comm's prepaid recharge services, plans, and offers. Get instant solutions for your queries.

1. How do I recharge my prepaid mobile on Mobi Comm?

Simply enter your mobile number, select your operator, choose a plan, and proceed with payment. Your recharge will be processed instantly.

2. What payment methods are accepted for recharges?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets like Paytm, Google Pay, and PhonePe.

3. How can I check my recharge history?

Log in to your Mobi Comm account, go to 'My Recharges,' and view your past transactions along with their status.

4. What should I do if my recharge fails?

If your recharge fails but the amount is deducted, it will be refunded within 24 hours. You can also contact our support team for assistance.

5. Are there any special offers or cashback on recharges?

Yes! We offer exclusive discounts, promo codes, and cashback offers on selected prepaid recharges. Check our 'Offers' section for details.
